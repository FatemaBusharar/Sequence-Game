Getting Started for Players

Learn how to play and master the Sequence board

1

Open the Game

  • Launch the Sequence game
  • Choose Two Player or Multiplayer
  • Each player enters a name that represents them
2

Set Up Your Hand

  • Each player receives 7 cards
  • Review your hand carefully
  • The game selects the first turn
3

Take Your Turn

  • Select a card from your hand
  • Place it on the board
  • Create a row of four cards
4

Block Opponents

  • Observe opponent moves
  • Block their sequences
  • Protect your progress
5

Draw a New Card

  • Draw a new card
  • Keep your hand full
  • Prepare for the next turn
